Andrew Roberts

Andrew Roberts is a certified teacher of Shaolin Cosmos Chi Kung as part of the Shaolin Wahnam Institute. He has been practicing the Shaolin Arts since 2005 and has learned from Sifu Wong in South America, the United States and Malaysia.

He is the coordinator of Chi Kung classes and healing sessions for Shaolin Wahnam California and has organized courses for Sifu Wong in California and South America.

Sifu Wong Kiew Kit

Grandmaster Wong, popularly known as Sifu Wong, is a world renowned teacher of the Shaolin Arts, namely Kung Fu, Chi Kung and Zen. Sifu Wong has been teaching for over 30 years and he regularly travels around the world spreading these precious arts to help people restore good health, vitality and longevity in their lives.

In 1997 he was recognized as "Chi Kung Master of the Year" at the World Chi Kung Congress in San Francisco. He has written numerous books on the Shaolin arts, including The Art of Chi Kung, The Complete Book of Tai Chi Chuan, The Art of Shaolin Kung Fu and The Complete Book of Chinese Medicine.
